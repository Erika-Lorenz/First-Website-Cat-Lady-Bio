Musings of A Dedicated Cat Lady

I'm the cat lady.

I have always had cats in my house, from the time I was in a crib. I became known as the cat whisperer in my family, and have more patience with these small demons than with anything else.

I guess you could say my cat lady afflication is partially genetic. However, my mother was more of a caregiver to any animal in need. She has a way of finding things to care for, and nourishing them back to health. Of course, by then she is too attached to consider anywhere else as a permanent home for such a soul.

Anyways, I seem to have specialised in cats. I bottle fed sick kittens, nursed injuries, and when I wasn't getting enough work at home (with up to 7 cats at a time!) I decided to volunteer at shelters.

At present, I have two cats at my apartment in Richmond, Nala and Appa. Although they will be a focal point on this website, I have also chosen to include the cats who I hold dear to my heart that do not live with me and those who have passed on.

If you have a love of cats, or even a fondness of animals in general, I hope you find this website fun, if not interesting. It is my first webpage created from scratch, and I wanted something I'm passionate about to be the focus, to help me get through learning HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

Thank you, and enjoy.
